8/21-8/24 Week 1:


This week we did a lot of setting up our website and now we're actually coding! I coded in html a long time ago and this has been a good refresher. We also learned some safety as well as the engineering process. Today specifically, we got to do a challenge where we had to take a marble from one point to another without it dropping on the floor, the only problem was that we didn't have enough track. So, our team came up with the plan that right after the marble traveled off our track, we'd run to the other end and give it to a person without a piece of track. We found that that strategy worked quite well, but we needed practice with it because people were holding it too steep and it sped up the marble to fast and it was uncontrollable. As a team, we managed to get the marble to the point we wanted it to get to a total of four times! :)
